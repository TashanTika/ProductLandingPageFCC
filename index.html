Features

 Samsung Galaxy A71 has finally released with Samsung Galaxy A51 in the month of December 2019. The Galaxy A71 comes with a bigger screen 6.7 inches Super AMOLED capacitive touchscreen with Corning Gorilla Glass 3. The Galaxy A71 is integrated with two variants such as 6 GB RAM with 128 GB internal storage and another one is 8 GB RAM with 128 GB internal storage. Although, the internal storage can be increased with the help of a microSD card up to 1 TB. The smartphone feature a quad camera on the rear side 64 MP (wide) + 12 MP (ultrawide) + 5 MP (wide) dedicated macro camera + 5 MP depth sensor. The front camera there is a 32 MP (wide) for clicking super selfies that feature HDR. There is an active noise cancellation with a dedicated mic along with a 3.5mm jack. It also features GPS, NFC (market dependent), Bluetooth 5.0, FM Radio, and Type C USB. It is fueled with a non-removable Li-Po 4500 mAh battery that provides Fast battery charging 25W. The Samsung Galaxy A71 runs on the Android 10.0 operating system with One UI 2. There are various colors available such as Prism, Crush Black, Silver, Blue, and Pink.

Performance & Specs
There's plenty of power under the hood of the Samsung Galaxy A71, with an octa-core processor and 6GB of RAM (some regions will get 8GB) in the driving seat. The device comes running Android 10, which felt fluid and fast under-finger, and you also get 128GB of storage to play with which, for many, will be more than enough. If you do run out of space, the Galaxy A71 also has a microSD slot which supports cards up to 512GB in size.
